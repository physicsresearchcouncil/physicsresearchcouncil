### 2. Round Description (930 Characters)
**Recommended Input:**
```text
Round 3 is the final theoretical round of the Physics Championship, testing advanced mathematical derivations and deep physical reasoning.

Submission Guidelines:
* Format: Candidates must download the question paper, write out their complete solutions and proofs by hand on blank sheets of paper, and upload a single, clearly scanned PDF document before the deadline.
* Duration: 3 hours (180 minutes) to solve and submit.
* Syllabus: Challenging, multi-stage problems covering Lagrangian mechanics, electrodynamics, thermodynamics, wave interference, and special relativity.
* Permitted Materials: Candidates are allowed one double-sided A4 formula sheet (formulas only, no worked solutions) and a non-programmable calculator.
* Evaluation: The exam is worth 100 marks. Graders will evaluate the logical progression, mathematical proofs, and physical principles demonstrated, rather than just the final answer.
```
