# Unstop Round 2 Configuration Details

This document contains the exact inputs for configuring Round 2 of the competition on the Unstop portal.

---

### 1. Round Name
**Recommended Input:**
```text
Round 2: Innovation & Research Portfolio
```

---

### 2. Round Description (890 Characters)
**Recommended Input:**
```text
Round 2 is an optional portfolio submission designed to reward candidates who have pursued independent STEM projects, science fair initiatives, computational models, or literature reviews.

Submission Guidelines:
* Permitted Formats: A single PDF document or a direct text link to a project repository (such as GitHub or a personal website).
* Eligible Submissions: You may upload a research summary, a physics simulation code (e.g., VPython, MATLAB), photos of an experimental setup with a brief description, or an academic Curriculum Vitae (CV) highlighting your past scientific engagements and accomplishments.
* Scoring Impact: This round is completely optional. Submissions will be evaluated by the Scientific Advisory Committee for up to 25 bonus marks, which are added directly to your final cumulative score.
* Evaluation Criteria: Projects are graded based on innovation, scientific rigor, and clarity of presentation.
```
