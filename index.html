Annual Event

The Physics Championship

A premier competition designed to discover exceptional physics talent across secondary and higher education brackets. Choose your path through our three distinct rounds.

Round 1 - Foundation

Conceptual Quiz Challenge

An entry-level round consisting of conceptual, multiple-choice, and short numerical physics problems. Designed to test logic, dimensional analysis, and foundational knowledge of physical principles.

  • 20 Questions in 60 minutes
  • Topics: Mechanics, Thermodynamics, Basic Electromagnetism
  • Fully online submission portal
  • Serves as the qualifying round for final honors
📝

Physics Fundamentals

A timed assessment that tests analytical clarity and quantitative speed.

60m

Time Limit

20

Total Qs

Round 2 - Creative (Optional)

Research & Showcase Portfolio

An optional submission round designed to reward students who have engaged in independent projects, science fairs, coding models, or literature reviews. Submit your academic CV or active research paper for bonus scoring.

  • Completely optional (serves as a grade booster)
  • Accepts: Written papers, coding scripts, physical models, or CVs
  • Peer-reviewed by our Advisory Board of professors
  • Great way to stand out for elite recognition levels
📁

Highlight your research, science fair projects, computational models, or coding repositories.

Round 3 - Mastery

Olympiad Theory Challenge

The ultimate test of physical reasoning. This round features proof-based, derivation-heavy, and complex multi-layered physics problems similar to international olympiads (IPhO, USAPhO, APhO).

  • 5-8 Complex multi-stage derivation questions
  • Calculus and advanced vector methods allowed
  • Scored on rigorous mathematical steps and physical intuition
  • Determines Gold, Silver, and Bronze Council medals
🌌

Theoretical Physics

Rigorous proof-based and calculational mechanics, electrodynamics, and relativity problems.
